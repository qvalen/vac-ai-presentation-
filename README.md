# VAC-AI Presentation

**Travel Planning Concept – Canva Prototype**

VAC-AI is a travel planning concept presented through a Canva prototype. This project showcases research, problem analysis, proposed solutions, and design ideas for a travel planning tool. The focus is on understanding user pain points, ideating solutions, and presenting visual prototypes.

## Problem Statement
VAC-AI’s promising start in the hospitality industry has soured due to declining sales and increased customer complaints. The presentation identifies challenges such as:  

- User dissatisfaction  
- Revenue loss  
- Uncertainty among employees due to rumors of mergers or sales  

Addressing these challenges requires:  

- Analyzing user feedback  
- Adjusting features for better usability  
- Stabilizing employee morale  
- Restoring customer confidence  


## Key Issues / Research Questions
1. **User Satisfaction:** How do user reviews reflect satisfaction and experience, especially compared to competitors?  
2. **Usability Issues:** What pain points do users face, and how do they affect engagement?  
3. **Value Proposition:** How does VAC-AI’s pricing and perceived value compare to alternatives, and how might this affect adoption?  


## Proposed Solutions
- **Solution 1:** Gather feedback on usability, features, and overall satisfaction to identify areas for improvement.  
- **Solution 2:** Address navigation complexity, lack of personalization, technical glitches, and customer support gaps.  
- **Solution 3:** Evaluate and adjust pricing and value proposition regularly based on market feedback.
